2018 Penn Aerospace Pitch Competition Application

Please submit a 1-2 page executive summary of your business plan and fill out your team contact info below to enter into the competition. Teams of 1-5 undergraduate, graduate, and/or postgraduate students may apply, contact pennaeropitch@gmail.com if you wish to apply but have a larger team.

All teams (at least one member per team) must be able to travel to the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, PA to present their entry on Saturday, February 24th, 2018 in the event they are selected as a finalist. If a team is found to be unable to appear on the day of the competition after being selected as a finalist, they may be disqualified and replaced by another applicant.

Please contact pennaeropitch@gmail.com with any questions or concerns.
