Penn Aerospace Pitch Competition

The Penn Aerospace Pitch Competition is a contest for enterprising students to present aerospace startup ideas at the University of Pennsylvania on February 25th, 2017.

Learn

Learn from aerospace industry professionals and keynotes who will join us on Penn's campus to talk about innovation in the aerospace industry and the budding opportunuties for entrepreneurs.

Innovate

Team up with aspiring entrepreneurs to create new ideas to disrupt aerospace. Ideas may range from aviation to drone delivery systems; from ISS supply logistics to new satellite applications.

Pitch

After qualifying pitch with your team in front of a panel of judges, including key industry professionals, for feedback and the chance to win up to $5,000.

Connect

Join a community of entrepreneurs, investors, industry professionals, students, and professors. The pitch competition will provide an optimal setting to share ideas, get feedback, and network.

Rules

Proposals must solve a problem related to air or space. Undergraduate and graduate students in teams of 1-5 must submit 1-2 page executive summaries by February 3rd to participate. Five teams will advance as finalists in the pitch competition.
Timeline

Executive Summaries due by Friday, February 3rd, 11:59pm ET
Qualifying Teams Notified on Monday, February 6th
Pitch Competition takes place on Saturday, February 25th, 10:20am - 12:00pm ET
Prizes

1st Place wins $5,000
2nd Place wins $2,500
3rd Place wins $1,000
